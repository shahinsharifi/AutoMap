Drawing Bounding box

This task is about annotating business-related store fronts on Street-Level images


1. First, you need to activate annotation mode by pressing the 'Start Annotation' button:

2. Once you activated annotation mode, you should accurately draw a bounding box around the store front on the image by holding the left button of you mouse. For Example:


NOTICE: It is possible that the width of image is bigger than your screen size. In that case, you should use the horizontal scroll bar to explore the whole image

Choosing label

3. As soon as drawing a bounding bow, the following window will be opened which asks you to choose the type of store-front:

4. By clicking on the drop down button, a list of different business types will be shown and you should choose the corresponding type correctly:

5. After choosing the type of business from drop down list, press 'Save' button to close the window.

Submit your answers

6. As there are usually more than one store-front on the image, you need to make sure that all visible store-fronts are annotated and listed in the table below the image:

7. In case of any mistake, you can remove your annotation record by pressing delete button (delete_outline) on the table.


8. When you are sure about your annotation records, press 'Submit' button on the bottom-right corner of the page and save your annotations.

 Finish the task

9. Once you submit your annotations records, the following page will be shown which means you have submitted your task successfully:

10. Copy generated token from the gray box(see above) and paste it to the very bottom of page:

11. Validate your token and then, press 'Finish' button to close the task. Please note that the payment will be done only after pressing the 'Finish' button.
